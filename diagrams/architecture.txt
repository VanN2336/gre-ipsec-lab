GRE over IPSec Lab Architecture

| left namespace              |             | right namespace             |

| veth-left: 192.168.100.1    |<----------->| veth-right: 192.168.100.2   |

Underlay network:
192.168.100.1 <-> 192.168.100.2

Tunnel network:
10.0.0.1 <-> 10.0.0.2

Protection:
GRE protocol 47 traffic is encrypted with IPSec ESP using Linux XFRM.
